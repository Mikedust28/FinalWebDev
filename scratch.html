About Us

Formed in Las Vegas, Palmasola is a group of high school friends who shared a love for music. Known to get together on occasion to jam, they decided to play a house show at frontman Michael Dustin's house. This show was called Music House and the Band's name at the time was The Dropouts. It was a great success with an attendance over 120 attendees, but the band shortly after disbanded due to each of the band members deciding to serve missions for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. In 2025, after their return, the band was reformed under the name Palmasola.
